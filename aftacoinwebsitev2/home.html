Executive Summary

AFTA Coin is a blockchain-based cryptocurrency designed to become the universal trade currency across Africa, promoting African unity, free trade, and financial inclusion. Inspired by the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA), AFTA Coin seeks to eliminate cross-border trade barriers, reduce remittance costs, and empower African SMEs and individuals.
