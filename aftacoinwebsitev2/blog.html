AFTACOIN: Fueling the AfCFTA with a Single Currency

Posted by Admin on Sep 10, 2025

The African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA) is a landmark agreement designed to create a single market for goods and services across Africa. However, the presence of **54 different currencies** remains a major obstacle. This currency fragmentation creates complexity, increases transaction costs, and complicates cross-border payments for businesses and individuals alike. AFTACOIN offers a powerful solution. By providing a secure, low-cost digital currency, we can facilitate seamless transactions, making it easier for SMEs to trade and grow without the friction of multiple exchange rates and high fees. A single digital currency can act as the monetary backbone for the AfCFTA, unlocking its full potential and truly uniting the continent's markets.
